About YieldFarm

Pioneering DeFi Since 2017

YieldFarm Platform was established in 2017 as one of the pioneering yield farming protocols in the decentralized finance space. Our vision has always been to democratize access to high-yield investment opportunities that were previously available only to institutional investors and high-net-worth individuals.

Backed by industry leaders including Binance, KuCoin, Bitget, Gate.io, MEXC, OKX, and ByBit, we have built a robust infrastructure that turns your USDT investments into exceptional returns. Our yield-farmed funds are strategically deployed across premier DeFi protocols including Jupiter, Aave, PancakeSwap, Orca, Radium, and SushiSwap, providing liquidity and automated market making while generating sustainable yields for our investors.
